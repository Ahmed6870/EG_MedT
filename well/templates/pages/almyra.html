Overview

Almyra is a luxurious, ocean-front hotel located on the beautiful island of Cyprus. It is situated in the heart of Paphos with the buzz of the harbour on its doorstep. Almyra which means ‘taste of the sea’ is a contemporary and stylish hotel, as you would expect from any member of Design Hotels. The focus here is on relaxation. This is especially true in the adults-only spa with its picture-perfect infinity swimming pool, as well as it's variety of spa treatments and therapies. If you’re looking for a sophisticated getaway by the emerald ocean, Almyra is the best choice.

**Almyra is closed for winter season from 24 November until 15 March 2024 **

When to go

The Almyra enjoys a typical Mediterranean climate. The average summer temperature is 30 degrees with July and August being the hottest months. The average winter temperate is 16 degrees and the coolest months are January and February.

Wellness

The adults-only* spa is spread over two floors. It’s sophisticated and sleek offering pampering treatments and holistic therapies. The focus is to allow you to relax and recharge your batteries. Organic ILA products are used as well as ingredients from the Almyra spa garden. Keep active with a rooftop yoga class, Pilates, or a PT session. A nutritionist is also on hand to support your wellness goals in your chosen package, including fitness and weight loss, sleep enhancement and relaxation. 
Facilities include 11 treatment rooms, indoor swimming pool, outdoor infinity pool, sauna, steam room, state-of-the-art gym, relaxation room, hair salon, beauty salon and Eauzone restaurant.


*Please note that children are allowed to use indoor pool in case of bad weather.

Rooms

There are 189 rooms and suites at the Almyra ranging from Inland Garden View rooms to the luxurious Kyma Suites which have private rooftop terraces. Each room and suite is bright and spacious with floor-to-ceiling patio windows and comfortable furnishings. Most of the rooms are located in the main building with the Kyma Suites located separately, closer to the spa. The Aethon Premium Sea View rooms are located in the adults-only wing and closer to the spa; ideal for spa guests. 


Inland View room

The Inland Garden View rooms are 28m2 and they overlook gardens. They each have a double bed or twin beds. These rooms do not have a balcony. 

Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer. 

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water on arrival, bathroom toiletries, bathrobe, slippers, telephone, tea & coffee making facilities and nightly turn down service.

Inland View room with Terrace

These rooms are 28m2 with a 9m2 private terrace which opens to shady garden. They have a double bed or twin beds. 

Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water on arrival, bathroom toiletries, bathrobe, slippers, telephone, tea & coffee making facilities and nightly turn down service.

Superior Sea View

Superior Sea View rooms are 28m2 and overlook the sea. They each have a king-size or twin beds. These rooms have floor to ceiling windows but no balcony. 

Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water on arrival, bathroom toiletries, bathrobe, slippers, telephone, tea & coffee making facilities and nightly turn down service.

Veranda Sea View

These contemporary Veranda Sea View rooms are 28m2 with a 4.5m2 private balcony overlooking the sea. They each have a king-size or twin beds. 

Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water on arrival, bathroom toiletries, bathrobe, slippers, telephone, tea & coffee making facilities and nightly turn down service.

Aethon - Premium Sea View

The Aethon rooms are 32m2 with a 5.5m2 private balcony with sea views towards Paphos Harbour. They are located near Almyra Spa in Almyra's adults-only wing. They have king-size beds. 

Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water, wine and fruit on arrival, bathroom toiletries and telephone. Tea & coffee making facilities are on request.

Terrace Sea View

The Terrace Sea View rooms are 28m2 and are located on the ground floor. They lead directly out to a private terrace with armchairs and sun-loungers. Each has a king-size or twin beds. 
Each has an en-suite bathroom with shower, bathrobes, slippers and hairdryer.

Facilities include a flat screen TV, Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, safe, minibar, bottle of water and fruit on arrival, bathroom toiletries and telephone. Tea & coffee making facilities are on request.

What’s on the menu

Almyra has a range of delicious cuisine options including traditional Cypriot meze, Japanese food and international cuisine. If you are opt for the half-board basis, you’ll dine from the Mediterranean and International buffet. The adults-only 'Eauzone' restaurant located in the spa serves healthy nibbles as well as juices and teas and is open for breakfast and lunch, in the summer months only. In the evening grab a 'mocktail' from the bar and watch the sunset on one of the comfy sofas overlooking the ocean and Paphos harbour. 
Mosaics
The main restaurant serving buffet breakfast and dinner with seasonal local produce, Cypriot pastries, Asian specialities and International food. If you’re on half board, this is where you will dine.
Notios
A Japanese-Mediterranean fusion serving a la carte menus. They serve lunch and dinner (March-December, weather permitting)
Ouzeri
Located right infront of the ocean, Ouzeri serves traditional Cypriot Meze. There is live entertainment some evenings. This restaurant is only open during the summer season.

Location

Almyra is located directly by the sea with a boulevard separating the hotel and the ocean. Paphos Harbour is a few minutes’ walk away with it's souvenir shops, restaurants and bars, ideal for al fresco dining. The beach in front of Almyra is small, however there are larger, sandy beaches further along the coast, such as coral bay, accessible by bus.

Nearest airport

Paphos Airport is a 20 minute drive from the hotel. Larnaca Airport is a 2 hour drive from the hotel. Flights to Paphos and Larnaca are very frequent and take approximately 5 hours from London.
