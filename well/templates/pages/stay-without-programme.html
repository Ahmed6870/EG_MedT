Overview hotel

Almyra is located directly by the sea with a boulevard separating the hotel and the ocean. Paphos Harbour is a few minutes’ walk away with it's souvenir shops, restaurants and bars, ideal for al fresco dining. The beach in front of Almyra is small, however there are larger, sandy beaches further along the coast, such as coral bay, accessible by bus.

When to go

The Almyra enjoys a typical Mediterranean climate. The average summer temperature is 30 degrees with July and August being the hottest months. The average winter temperate is 16 degrees and the coolest months are January and February.
